<div class="row">

<div class="col-md-7" style="margin-bottom: 1em;">

<p><strong>Contact Me</strong>: I am the subject librarian for 
<a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/subjects/computer-science">Computer Science</a>, <a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/subjects/information-systems">Information Systems</a>, and <a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/subjects/mathematics">Mathematics</a>. <p><strong>Online Office Hours</strong>: My office hours are 10:30 &ndash; 12:00 and 1:30 &ndash; 3:00 during the week. You can schedule an appointment to meet with me <a href="https://lemoyne.libcal.com/appointments/tomkeays/in-person">in-person</a> or <a href="https://lemoyne.libcal.com/appointments/tomkeays/zoom">online using Zoom</a> during these times.</p>

<p><strong>Alternative Contact Methods</strong>: My work schedule is posted in the calendar block on this page. Each day will list whether I am working on-campus or remotely. If I&#39;m listed in the calendar as being on-call, then I am available using the library&#39;s <a href="https://resources.library.lemoyne.edu/askus/by-chat">chat service</a>. That&#39;s a good way to reach me to get a question answered quickly or to arrange an alternate time to meet if my scheduled office hours are not convenient. You can also reach me by email and I&#39;ll reply during my working hours.</p>
